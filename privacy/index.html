Privacy Policy

Transparent. Simple. Secure.

Data Collection

Chooser does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive data. We believe in complete privacy - your information stays on your device, period.

Network Access

The app does not access the internet or communicate with developers or any third parties. All functionality works completely offline, ensuring your privacy is never compromised.

Open Source

Chooser is open source software. You can review the complete source code on our GitHub repository to verify these privacy claims yourself. Transparency is key to trust.

How It Works

All data processing happens locally on your device. No analytics, no tracking, no telemetry. The app functions entirely within your system's boundaries.

Policy Updates

This privacy policy is effective as of July 2025. Any future updates will be posted on this page and in the app's GitHub repository. We will never change our core commitment to privacy.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the Chooser app, feel free to reach out through our GitHub repository or contact us directly.
